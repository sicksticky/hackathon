Food wastes from individual families contribute alot to the total food waste.

We can all do a little and donate our extra food, every change starts from the smallest unit.

Kindly join us and bring out food to any food banks around you, click here! to see the map.

Here are some tips to reduce our food waste:

  1. Shop smart: make plans and grocery list so we canbuy exactly what we need and avoid impulse buys.
  2. First in, First out: when unpacking groceries, move older products to the front and put new products to the back
  3. Store and eat leftovers: try storing food in airtight containers and keep for next lunch
  4. Donate anything!: anything from canned food to food scraps can be donated. Edible food can be given to the hungries and farmers would ahppily accept the food scraps for fertilisers
